## Common Scenarios

### "Someone cheated me, took money and didn't return"
‚Üí Section 420 IPC (Cheating - 7 years imprisonment)

### "Wife seeking money from husband"
‚Üí Section 125 CrPC (Maintenance - up to Rs. 10,000/month)

‚Üí Section 66C IT Act (Identity Theft - 3 years + Rs. 1 lakh fine)

### "Police arrested me, how long can they keep me?"
‚Üí Section 57 CrPC (Maximum 24 hours without magistrate order)

### "I want information from government office"
‚Üí Section 6 RTI Act (File RTI application, no reason needed)

‚Üí Section 185 MV Act (Blood alcohol >30mg/100ml = 6 months + Rs. 2,000)

### "Product is defective, want refund"
‚Üí Section 87 Consumer Act (Can claim refund/replacement/compensation)

### "Contract signed under pressure"
‚Üí Section 15 Contract Act (Coercion makes contract voidable)

### "Someone stole my laptop"
‚Üí Section 379 IPC (Theft - imprisonment up to 3 years)

### "Person threatening to harm me"
‚Üí Section 506 IPC (Criminal intimidation - up to 7 years if threat of death)
