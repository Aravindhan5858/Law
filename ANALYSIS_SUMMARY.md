## üìö APPLICABLE LEGAL SECTIONS

### 1. IPC Section 420 - Cheating
**Punishment:** Up to 7 years imprisonment + Fine  
**Applies when:** Seller intentionally takes payment but fails to deliver

### 2. IPC Section 406 - Criminal Breach of Trust
**Punishment:** Up to 3 years imprisonment + Fine  
**Applies when:** Money entrusted for goods not delivered/returned

### 3. IT Act Section 66D - Cyber Cheating
**Punishment:** Up to 3 years imprisonment + Fine up to Rs. 1 lakh  
**Applies when:** Computer/website used to commit fraud

### 4. Consumer Protection Act - Deficiency in Service
**Remedy:** Refund + Compensation + Interest  
**Applies when:** Service provider fails to deliver promised goods

### 5. Consumer Protection Act - Unfair Trade Practice
**Remedy:** Refund + Punitive damages  
**Applies when:** Deceptive practices, non-performance of promise

### 6. Contract Act Section 73 - Breach of Contract
**Remedy:** Compensation for loss and damage  
**Applies when:** Contract breached by non-delivery
